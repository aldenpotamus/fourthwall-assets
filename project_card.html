Visual Guide to Base Building

This new page is an interactive guide to building efficient and easily defendable bases. It allows the user to scan through many several build options (corpse based strategies and production based ones), and see both in-game screenshots and a grid based view!

This is an ongoing project that will hopefully develop into more of a "map guide" style site giving players all the critical information they need to approach maps with confidence!

Damage Calc Spreadsheet

This spreadsheet and the accompanying videos are designed to help demystify the nature of damage calculations in The Last Spell. The nature of the game doesn't always allow you as a player the agency to "What-if" builds to determine where you might have went wrong. This means that it's easy as a player to attribute success to bad decisions or completely miss out on potential improvement when things "are good enough".

The sheet provides you as the player the ability to rewind time and try multiple paths, or simply enter your hero data and let the sheet guide you to greatness.

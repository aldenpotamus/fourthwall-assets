Games

The Last Spell is a cornerstone of the channel, this grid based indie roguelike captured my imagination and interest the moment I laid eyes on it. The community has collectively authored a variety of mods for the game as well as dreaming up all manner of diabolical challenges to undertake.

As a huge fan of the original hit of the same name, Darkest Dungeon II has captured something different but also special. The original's long campaign has been replaced by a more "snack-able" adventure with some hero carry over between runs. While perhaps less iconic than its predecessor, DD2 still has an addictive quality and development is still ongoing.
